Headline Articles

Headline articles, linked from your Player News page, are important messages from the admins, or from the Acutron itself. Find out about just-introduced mind-blasting technologies, newly-released upgrades, and game winners.

If a game's schedule has to be changed for some reason, or if there are other changes you need to know about, they'll appear here.

To view an article, click the headline.

You can also subscribe to an RSS feed that will bring you the headlines – just click the rss link in the Headlines section.

Headlines in PUB Games

Headlines work a bit differently in PUB Games. Any player in the game can post a headline for all other players to read. Thus, the headlines become that game's private news area.
