June 24, 2008: Zenrin Art Of Silence Lock

All Zenrin may now lock 5 Art of Silence at one time. (Other races may only lock one at a time.) This is effective immediately on all games.

June 8, 2008: Starcharger Starts Trouble!!!

Our next mega game is now open: Trouble! This is another classic game with a drifty map, and 252 homeworlds. The first tick is at 9 PM on Wednesday.

We have made a few minor race and unit changes; see the forum thread for details. Oh, and take note: all nobody worlds will start building units beginning on turn 10.

Starcharger is helping us test some of the new admin features, and he set up the new map. One of the perks to starting games is adding worlds... Look for Starchargerville. ;)
