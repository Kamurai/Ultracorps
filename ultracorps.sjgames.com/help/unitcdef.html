This lumbering colossus of the void is feared by all. With innumerable positron lasers, antimatter grenade launchers, incinerator beams, and ultranapalm emitters, even a single Planet Attacker poses a huge threat to any opposing force. Because its armor is pierced in so many places by weapon ports, the GPA is more vulnerable than most capital ships, and is best deployed with escorts to keep a lucky shot from detonating its whole weapon load. Therefore, its huge cargo bays allow it to carry an entire fleet of support units.

All Entradishar instinctively know how to build the Giganto Planet Attacker. License cost is 750, and CPX is 750.
