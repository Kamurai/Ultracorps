Watch List

This Com-Net tab takes you to a list of players that you want to keep a special eye on. At the beginning of the game, the only empire on the Watch List is your own. Any other player can be added to the Watch List by going to their Empire page and clicking the appropriate link.

You will probably want to add all your immediate neighbors to the list, plus any other players in which you have a special interest.

In a PUB game, the Watch List is automatically populated with all other players in the game.

This page has:

  • A table of basic stats like world count, FP, and population, to let you compare the success of empires on the list.
  • Links to let you view the player's empire and send UltraMail.
