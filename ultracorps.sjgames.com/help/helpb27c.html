Handing Off a Game to Another Player

If something happens to keep you from playing actively, and quitting would spoil the game for the other players, you can give a game position to another player. The "Handoff" link is located on your Empire Page.

If a game is being played for points:

  • The original player earns XP for the turns he played. The stand-in gets XP for the turns he plays.
  • If the handoff takes place on or before turn 3, the original player gets no MP and the game does not affect his average. The stand-in gets MP and the game affects his average normally.
  • If the handoff takes place on turn 4 or later, neither player gets MP and the game does not affect either one's average. (So yes, you could protect your average by giving a game away, at the cost of all the MP and XP you could have earned.)
