The ancient art of DinChoFu has been studied by the Zenrin for untold millennia. By developing the simple arts of breath, attention, and efficient movement, Zenrin war monks have acquired a host of bizarre unarmed abilities which can be most useful to a resourceful commander. The War Monk represents the lowest level of DinChoFu training. At this early level, the monk's skills are limited to unarmed fighting techniques and sabotage training. But if time and energy are invested to train the monk in the secret arts, his arcane skills can match even a fleet of warships.

Training in ZinCho Mystic Breath upgrades this humble monk to the Zenrin Fighter v2.
