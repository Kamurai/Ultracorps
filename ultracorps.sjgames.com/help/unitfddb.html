This huge energy cannon is the ultimate weapon of mass destruction. Anchored to a moon or uninhabited planet, the Orn Moon Gun delivers a blast of inversion beam energy capable of overwhelming the most hardened defense. Its own structure is nearly invulnerable. The OMG's drawback is its massive size; if not produced in the system where it is to be installed, a huge amount of interstellar towing capacity is required to move it.

The OMG has integral scanning capability up to 120 units.

Orn Moon Guns not in base fleet require lunar installation before activation; they will not fire (but may still be targeted) in the first three rounds of combat.

CPX for the Orn Moon Gun is halved for Orn players.
