May 27, 2009: Server Glitch

The Acutron blew a RAM chip early this morning, and went into sleep mode to regenerate. Things should be back to normal now. Tonight's Ynsanity tick will proceed as planned.

If your PUB game had ticks scheduled during the outage, you should delete those and adjust your game's tick schedule appropriately.


May 21, 2009: Show All Licenses

Ever had trouble remembering what world a certain license was on? We've solved that now with the "show all licenses" page. To view it, click on the "all production" icon at the top of your in-game navbar, and then click on "Show All Licenses".

May 11, 2009: Worlds Data Now Available

Actually it's been available since the start of Ynsanity, but you can now find data on all the worlds in your game by going to the "show all worlds" page (the world icon in the top frame). At the bottom of that page is a link to worlds.csv (for PUB games); Mega games have a separate csv file for each turn in the game. (Solo games don't have this file. Sorry.)

You can import the data into your favorite spreadsheet program to crunch numbers, or use it to create your own mapping program.

And remember there's also a units.csv file available on the units help page for each game.
