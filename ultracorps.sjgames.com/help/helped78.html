Avatars

Subscribers may add an avatar which will appear on their Profile page and on forum postings they make.

To change your avatar, go to your Profile page and click on it.

Avatars must be GIF or JPG images, up to 100 x 100 pixels in dimensions, and under 30KB in size. If you don't want to create an original avatar, you are welcome to draw one from the avatar library (to which contributions are welcome . . . send them to help@ultracorps.com). Please note that offensive avatars will be removed and the offending Commander's avatar privileges will be revoked.
