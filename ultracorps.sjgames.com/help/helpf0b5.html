Subscribing to UltraCorps

You can play at the basic level for free . . . and we want you to. Don't subscribe until you know you like the game!

But if you decide to keep playing, please consider subscribing, because it's the subscribers that make it possible for us to keep UltraCorps going and add new features.

Subscribers get extra game features, of course!
