The Xiron is a balance of attack strength and defensive endurance, with moderate speed and the ability to carry a complement of surface attack units. These ships are a useful addition to any fleet, and are famed for their ability to take punishment and keep fighting.

Adding the Xiron Force Coil - a license that is only available to Xirons - upgrades this unit to the Xiron Heavy Cruiser v2.
