This unit is created by upgrading a basic Boron Recon Buggy with the Boron 6-Wheel Rig.

The v2 Boron Recon Buggy, with improved suspension and armor and the redundancy of six wheels, is significantly tougher than the base model, with no loss in performance.

Adding the Boron Targeting Array further upgrades this unit to the Boron Recon Buggy v3.
